OVERVIEW
--------
SoilSense AI Ltd is a UK-based startup founded in 2021, headquartered in Norwich,
England. The company develops low-cost wireless soil health sensors combined with a
predictive analytics platform to track soil carbon sequestration, moisture, pH, and
nitrogen levels in real time.

Its hardware-software integrated offering targets both commercial farms and land
management organisations (e.g., Natural England, RSPB) seeking to quantify carbon
credits from regenerative agriculture practices. The company is pre-revenue on its
carbon credit verification module but has launched a commercial soil monitoring
subscription in early 2025.

ANALYST PRELIMINARY VIEW
------------------------
SoilSense is an exciting early-stage company in a rapidly growing niche (agri carbon).
Revenue growth is exceptional (+200%) but from a very small base. The hardware margin
drag and short runway are concerns. The Verra validation result will be a key binary
event. Monitor closely but wait for carbon module validation before committing.
