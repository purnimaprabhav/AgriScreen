OVERVIEW
--------
GreenYield Technologies BV is a Dutch agtech company founded in 2017, based in
Wageningen (Netherlands). The company builds crop-disease early-warning systems
using hyperspectral drone imaging and machine learning. Its platform, "CropGuard",
alerts farm managers 7-14 days before visible disease symptoms appear, enabling
targeted fungicide application and reducing chemical use by up to 40%.

GreenYield serves primarily potato, sugar beet, and tulip growers in the Netherlands,
Belgium, and Germany. It also has a growing presence in Eastern Europe (Poland, Czech
Republic) via a reseller agreement signed in late 2024.
