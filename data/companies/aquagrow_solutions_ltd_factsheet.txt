OVERVIEW
--------
AquaGrow Solutions Ltd (formerly AquaAg Technologies) is an Israeli agricultural
water management company founded in 2014 and headquartered in Tel Aviv. The company
provides precision irrigation management systems combining AI-driven evapotranspiration
modelling, drip irrigation hardware, and a farm management application.

AquaGrow serves over 3,400 farms across Israel, Spain, Morocco, and California (US),
predominantly in high-value crops: avocado, citrus, tomato, and table grapes.
The company rebranded in 2023 following its acquisition of Spanish irrigation hardware
maker TechRiego SL, which significantly expanded its hardware capabilities and Iberian
market presence.
