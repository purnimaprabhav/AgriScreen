OVERVIEW
--------
Verdant Farms SA is a French precision agriculture company founded in 2019 and
headquartered in Montpellier, France. The company develops an AI-powered crop
management platform that integrates satellite imagery, IoT soil sensors, and
weather forecasting to provide real-time agronomic recommendations to farmers.

Its flagship product, "AgroLens", is a SaaS platform used by over 1,200
commercial farms across France, Spain, and Italy, covering approximately 420,000
hectares of cultivated land as of Q4 2025.
