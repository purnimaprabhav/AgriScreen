[NEWS-009] — 4 April 2026
VERDANT FARMS: SERIES C PREPARATION RUMOURED
Multiple market sources suggest Verdant Farms SA is preparing for a Series C
fundraising round targeting EUR 30-40M. The company has reportedly engaged
Rothschild & Co as financial advisor. A formal process is expected to launch
in Q3 2026. Potential investors cited include Demeter Investment Managers and
AXA Climate. The Crédit Agricole LOI conversion is likely to be a key proof
point in the fundraising narrative.
Source: AgFunder News (unconfirmed); Dealroom.co monitoring alert.

[NEWS-010] — 16 April 2026
AQUAGROW SOLUTIONS: STRATEGIC ACQUISITION TALKS CONFIRMED
AquaGrow Solutions CEO Oded Katz confirmed in an interview with The Times of
Israel that the company has received "multiple expressions of interest from
strategic players." He declined to name the interested parties but stated the
company is "evaluating all options to maximise shareholder value." Analysts
believe Valmont Industries (US) and a European agri-input distributor are
the most likely acquirers. Any transaction is expected to value AquaGrow at
USD 90-120M, representing a 3-5x revenue multiple.
Source: The Times of Israel interview, April 16; Bloomberg Terminal.

[NEWS-011] — 29 April 2026
HARVESTLINK GMBH: STRATEGIC SALE MANDATE CONFIRMED
HarvestLink GmbH officially confirmed it has engaged an M&A advisor (believed
to be Lincoln International's Frankfurt office) to explore strategic options
including a full sale, merger, or capital injection. Management stated they
"continue to have constructive conversations with several interested parties."
Given the company's 11-month runway and declining revenues, observers expect
the process to conclude by Q3 2026. Potential acquirers include SAP SE, which
has been building an agricultural supply chain software stack, and Agrifirm
(Dutch cooperative giant).
Source: HarvestLink press release; AgFunder News; Bloomberg.

[NEWS-012] — 12 May 2026
GREENYIELD TECHNOLOGIES: SERIES B PROCESS TO LAUNCH Q3 2026
GreenYield Technologies confirmed in a LinkedIn post by CEO Pieter van den Berg
that the company will formally launch its Series B fundraising process in
September 2026, targeting EUR 12-15M. Van den Berg stated: "The Cosun pilot
results, expected in Q4 2026, will be a strong validation point. We will enter
the fundraising process with a compelling commercial story and a clear path to
profitability." Existing investors Anterra Capital and Rabobank are expected
to participate in the round.
Source: LinkedIn post (GreenYield Technologies official page); AgInvestor.

[NEWS-013] — 20 May 2026
BIOROOT INNOVATIONS: SPANISH REGULATORY APPROVAL DELAYED BY 4 MONTHS
BioRoot Innovations SA announced that the Spanish MITERD (Ministry for Ecological
Transition) has extended its review period for RhizoBoost by four months, citing
the need for additional ecotoxicology data. The approval, previously expected in
Q3 2026, is now anticipated for Q4 2026 at the earliest, with a risk of slipping
to Q1 2027. BioRoot's CEO stated the company is "fully cooperating with the
regulatory process" and does not expect the delay to materially impact FY2026
revenues, as Spain represented a smaller portion of near-term commercial projections.
However, analysts note this could delay Italian submission timelines as well.
Source: BioRoot Innovations press release; Spain MITERD official communication.
